My top 3 movies of all time

Aanand

This is a captivating movie about friendship. This movie shows the way to live the life.

Gangs of Wasseypur

Anurag Kashyap directed masterpiece is a great experience to watch. Story is based on gang wars in a city of Bihar.

Rockstar

Imtiaz Ali created this masterpiece to show that success and fame is not the same as it appears.
